## Tips

1. **Always backup** before editing game files
2. **Use UPPERCASE filenames** for VXL/HVA files
3. **Test frequently** - small changes, test often
4. **Check normals** in VXLSE III for proper lighting
5. **Match section names** between VXL and HVA files
